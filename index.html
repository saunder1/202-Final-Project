Scroll on to explore the rise and fall of a once-unrivalled competitive shooter.

Overwatch launches with 21 heroes you can play as, all with unique abilities and playstyles. There are now 37!

Overwatch releases in May 2016 and wins Game of the Year in December.

The Overwatch League's inaugral season is in January 2018, the first pro-gaming league of its kind.

Blizzcon 2019: Game Director Jeff Kaplan announces Overwatch 2!

Overwatch 2 will have a singleplayer campaign mode in addition to the competitive multiplayer.

The Campaign mode will have "endlessly replayable" hero-missions with hero skill trees allowing unique playthroughs.

There would be all new enemies to fight, new abilities, and new maps. Players that were not as interested in competitive PVP were very excited.

Then, blizzard goes quiet...
Although there are still many players, updates and new content for the game slows to a crawl...
supposedly, the developers are working hard on Overwatch 2...
finally, in march 2022, the release date for Overwatch 2 is announced!

OCTOBER 2022:

OW2 Releases

"Overwatch 2" is released but it is basically the same game but with a big balance update.

The game is now teams of 5 instead of 6 and some heroes have been changed.

The game should really be called Overwatch 1.5

Blizzard says that the PVE will come soon...

We wait for 6 months...
